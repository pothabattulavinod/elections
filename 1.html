About the Elections

Elections ensure democratic leadership and village development. Citizens choose a leader who manages welfare, development, and governance.

Election Process

1. Notification

Election authority announces the official schedule and rules.

2. Nominations

Candidates submit their applications and supporting documents.

3. Voting

Voters cast votes; the candidate with the most votes becomes Sarpanch.
