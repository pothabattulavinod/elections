About the Elections

Elections ensure democratic leadership and village development. They allow citizens to choose a leader who manages welfare, development, and governance.

Election Process

1. Notification

Election authority announces the official schedule and rules.

2. Nominations

Candidates submit their applications and supporting documents.

3. Voting

Voters cast votes; the candidate with the most votes becomes Sarpanch.

Roles & Responsibilities

Village Development

Roads, water supply, sanitation, schools and other infrastructure.

Financial Management

Budget preparation, accounts, and transparent fund management.

Welfare Schemes

Implementation of national & state welfare programs.
